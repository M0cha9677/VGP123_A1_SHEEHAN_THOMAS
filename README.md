# VGP123_A1_SHEEHAN_THOMAS
Select and play an NES game of choice. Analyze the mechanics in the game, paying attention to components, movement, and interactions. Record the different items that you will develop as part of your level, and have your instructor approve your choices. Recreate one level from the game and ensure it includes: • A level with at least 2000 pixels in width or height • Power-ups, collectibles, dynamic UI, title screens • Platforms • At least two types of enemies with simple AI • At least one collectible • At least one power-up • One obstacle that can affect the player (not an enemy or AI) You should develop: • Ability to play through the level • Character controls with movement, animation and collision • Collectibles with animation and collision Art assets and animations can be found at www.spriters- resource.com/nes
